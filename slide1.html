01. What is Accounting?

Accounting is the systematic process of recording, analyzing, and reporting financial transactions to provide accurate financial information for decision-making.

üîç Core Functions of Accounting

  • Recording: Systematic documentation of all financial transactions
  • Classifying: Organizing transactions into meaningful categories
  • Summarizing: Condensing financial data into useful reports
  • Interpreting: Analyzing data to provide insights and recommendations
  • Reporting: Communicating financial information to stakeholders

üéØ The Accounting Equation

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

This fundamental equation forms the foundation of double-entry bookkeeping and ensures that every transaction maintains the balance in financial records.
